Unveiling My Journey: From Code Enthusiast to Product Maestro.

I always thought my dream was to be just a Software engineer at a giant company, sipping my coffee and coding my way to the next app which people find the most convenient. It seemed like a perfect dream to start with a B.E in Computer Science & Technology. I am enthusiastic and excited about things in day-to-day life and was always fascinated by new gadgets, technology and trends. My curiosity is my strength it allows me to generate the tenacity and willingness to learn new things be it in professional and personal life. I gravitated toward marketing and product management and quality control positions, and I started to work for a company like this. But I kind of just felt meh. I yearned to learn more, yearned to learn something different. And that's when it happened; the spark I was missing ignited the instant I clicked play on my first Product Management video. (I mean seriously, how could it not be great when it's named after Monty Product Marketing School) Since then, I can't get enough. Product Management and Front-end web development calls to all my passions; It incorporates creativity and problem solving and I'm allowed to break it to improve the code (in a separate Gitbranch, of course). I also loved applying responsive design principles and watching my web pages shrink into mobile screens and still look amazing. It's oddly satisfying. I think in a way I'm kind of like those web pages, I'm moldable, but I keep my creative flare intact.
